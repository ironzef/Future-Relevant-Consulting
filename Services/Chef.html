Dal, Litti & Chokha

Con-Fused Litti Chokha from Jharkhand & Bihar and Dal Bati from Rajasthan. Our Litti Chokha is made with hand-pounded wheat flour and a filling of roasted gram flour and tangy spices, slow-cooked in a traditional clay oven with coal. The Chokha/Bhartu is a medley of roasted and mashed eggplant, potato, tomato, green chillies, and garlic. Our Dal Bati is made with boiled and baked wheat flour bread, and a fragrant Dal made with multiple lentils, spices, and fresh herbs, slow-cooked to perfection. Finally, soaked in suddh biloni organi ghee for an unforgettable culinary experience.

Price - Rs.300/Plate

Kachori Sabji

Con-Fused Kachori & Sabji combines the crispy and spicy fillings, accompanied by potatoes and tomatoes curry, flavoured by a homemade blend of roasted spices panchforan comes out of Con-Fusion of all-time favourite staple food from Rajasthan, UP, Jharkhand, Bihar and West Bengal, in three varieties of kachoris fillings of Sattu, Matar & Bedmi to choose from.

Price - Rs.250/Plate
